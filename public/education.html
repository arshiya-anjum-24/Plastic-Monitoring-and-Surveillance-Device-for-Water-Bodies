Our Mission

Learn more about the dangers of plastic pollution and how our project helps monitor and fight it.

  • Plastic pollution harms marine life and water ecosystems.
  • Our boat uses IoT and AI to detect plastic hotspots in real-time.
  • Heatmaps generated from GPS logs provide actionable insights.

About Us

We are a research-driven team building an AI-powered boat that detects plastic waste in rivers and lakes. Our goal is to provide real-time insights into pollution hotspots using robotics, IoT, and machine learning.
